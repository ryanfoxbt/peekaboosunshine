About

Peek-A-Who?

Peek-A-Boo Sunshine is a fun musical duo consisting of Ryan Fox and Joe Rogero. Joe & Ryan met on craigslist. The first encounter was in Ryan's poorly lit garage /spider farm after 18 months of social isolation. He didn't murder me. I didn't murder him so the meeting went well. We sounded terrible because the pandemic turned us into even MORE awkward mole people. But once we emerged from our mole holes and started looking into each other's beady sun deprived eyes we married our guitars, voices, & ideas together to give birth to Peekaboo Sunshine.

Peekaboo Sunshine's songwriting style is "a naked". We share our insecurities & vulerabilities with you via song (& sometimes dance) without bumming you out. Our arrangments are catchy- splashes of color with moments of dissonance to lure you in and fun you out.
